Phemex Review

This exchange is one of the newcomers to the space. Founded at the end of 2019, it is already looking quite impressive with everything it has achieved in such a short period of time. Of course, some things still need building out and improving, which I will cover as well.

The platform has already attracted over 5 million users globally and has a healthy 24-hour trading volume of over $92 million as of July 2023. Not bad, considering we are in the depths of a bear market.

Phemex is located and registered in Singapore and is owned by Phemex Pte. Ltd.

The platform was founded by a team made up of individuals who have decades of previous experience working with banking giant Morgan Stanley, as well as some old-school Bitcoin miners, providing the team with a great blend of experience from TradFi and Blockchain.

Additional Features

Phemex is a cryptocurrency exchange offering a range of innovative features and services, positioning itself as a versatile platform for traders and investors.

1. Derivatives Trading

Phemex is known for its derivatives trading platform, offering users access to perpetual futures markets with up to 100x leverage. This includes advanced order types and trading tools integrated with TradingView​.

2. Sub-Accounts

A standout feature of Phemex is its ability to create sub-accounts, allowing users to segregate their trading strategies across different accounts.

3. Earn Program

The Phemex Earn feature lets users earn interest on their crypto assets, offering up to 18.8% APY on short-term investments. It is a competitive offering but has limited asset choices​.

4. Launchpad and Staking

Phemex’s Launchpad allows users to participate in Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs), while LaunchPool enables staking for select tokens. However, both offerings are relatively limited in scope compared to other exchanges​.

5. Hybrid Exchange Model

Phemex is transitioning into a hybrid exchange model, blending centralized (CEX) and decentralized (DEX) elements. This includes governance through a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO), giving users more control over platform decisions​(.

6. Native Token (PT)

Phemex introduced its native token, PT, which offers benefits such as trading fee discounts, staking opportunities with high APR, and governance rights through the Phemex DAO.
