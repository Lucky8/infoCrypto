Additional Features

VALR is a leading cryptocurrency exchange based in South Africa, offering a variety of features that make it user-friendly and accessible for both beginners and advanced traders.

1. User-Friendly Interface

VALR's platform is designed to be intuitive, making it easy for users to navigate and trade cryptocurrencies. It offers both a basic Buy/Sell option for newcomers and a more advanced trading platform for seasoned investors​.

2. Fiat and Cryptocurrency Support

VALR allows users to fund their accounts using the South African Rand (ZAR) and a variety of cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and Ripple (XRP). ZAR deposits can be made through local bank transfers​.

3. Low Fees and Payment for Liquidity

VALR offers competitive fees, with a unique feature where market makers (those providing liquidity to the order books) are paid 0.01% of the trade value when their orders get matched​.

4. VALR Pay

This feature allows users to send crypto or cash to any mobile number, email address, or VALR Pay ID using a QR code. It makes peer-to-peer transactions fast and simple​.

5. Rapid Verification

VALR uses fast verification technology, streamlining the process for users to complete their KYC (Know Your Customer) procedures​

6. Portfolio Tracking

Users can monitor their portfolio performance in various reference currencies like USD, BTC, and ZAR, helping them keep track of profits and distribution​.

7. Educational Resources

While VALR lacks extensive educational tools or demo accounts, it provides an easy onboarding process for users to get started quickly.
