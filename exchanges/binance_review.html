Binance Review

Binance is a popular cryptocurrency exchange that was founded in 2017 by Changpeng Zhao (CZ). The platform provides trading opportunities for over 500+ cryptocurrencies, especially altcoins. It covers popular coins such as Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Litecoin (LTC), Dogecoin (DOGE), including its own blockchain-based token, Binance Coin (BNB).

Binance’s design allows for higher levels of processing throughput. The exchange is able to process around 1.4 million orders per second. In addition to exchange-specific services, Binance also offers other tools, investment options, platforms, and services for supporting the overall blockchain ecosystem. Binance also offers a Visa debit card, but Binance card reviews are middling. DeFi services include Binance Staking.

Binance Labs supports promising pre-ICO stage projects. Many newly created cryptocurrency projects can raise the necessary funds through the platform. Binance also provides advisory resources, and a launchpad for any project’s necessary listing and fundraising exercises.

Binance supports three types of trade orders: limit, market, and stop-limit orders. Limit orders are executed only at the limit price set by the trader, market orders are executed immediately at the best available market price, while stop limit orders become valid orders only when the price reaches a specified level.

Binance has several trade options and offers additional order types for crypto derivatives, specifically on futures contracts.

Additional Features

Binance, one of the largest cryptocurrency exchanges, has introduced a range of innovative features in recent years to maintain its leadership in the crypto space. Some of the key features and innovations include:

1. Web3 Wallet & Self-Custody

Binance launched a Web3 wallet in 2023, offering users self-custodial solutions for their digital assets. This has allowed millions of users to create Web3 wallets, providing them control over their private keys and improving asset security.

2. SocialFi Expansion

Binance has delved into the growing SocialFi (Social Finance) sector, emphasizing platforms like friend.tech, which blend social media with financial opportunities for creators to monetize their content. Although interest in these platforms has fluctuated, Binance sees potential for further growth in 2024.

3. Binance Square

This social content platform saw a ninefold increase in creators in 2023, offering users a space to engage with crypto-related content and share insights.

4. Binance Earn

The platform allows users to earn passive income through various crypto investment products. In 2023, Binance Earn saw a 35% rise in users and an increase in total value locked, reflecting growing interest in earning through DeFi products.

5. Compliance and Transparency

Binance has significantly ramped up its compliance efforts, investing $213 million in 2023 for regulatory initiatives, including enhanced market surveillance and proof-of-reserves reports, which now support 31 tokens​.

6. Bitcoin Innovations and Stablecoins

In 2024, Binance is focusing on trends like Bitcoin Ordinals and BRC-20 tokens, which allow more innovative uses for Bitcoin beyond transactions. The growth of stablecoins is also seen as a key liquidity indicator, potentially boosting market activity.
