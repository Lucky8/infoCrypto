EasyProperties Review

EasyProperties is an online platform offering investors access to fractionalised shares in new and existing property developments.

The purpose of the platform is to make property investment more accessible to a wider range of people by making it possible for investors of any experience level to gain access to high-quality investment properties that they are able to evaluate and and choose from, whereas in the past this opportunity was previously only available to wealthy individuals or individuals with access to large amounts of capital.

EasyProperties' founders comprise of a wide variety of partners, staff, and leaders in FinTech and real estate development to deliver a PropTech platform that serves to democratise property investing.

EasyProperties is a partnership between EasyEquities and Narrative, a leading South African property development group. Narrative possesses a wealth of experience in the residential property development space and has access to quality property opportunities on an ongoing basis.

All property opportunities offered on EasyProperties are subjected to our rigorous investment criteria and assessment process. As a result, investors can rest assured that the opportunities offered on EasyProperties have been thoroughly and expertly reviewed and offer attractive returns on a risk-adjusted basis.

Opening an Account on EasyProperties

To activate your EasyProperties you will need an EasyEquities account.

1. Log in to EasyEquities and click on the banner to activate your EasyProperties account.

2. Review the EasyProperties terms and conditions and click activate.

3. You can then log in to EasyProperties with your EasyID (your EasyEquities username and password).
